Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

3inOne Store (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the data you provide when using our website, social media pages, and messaging platforms.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, contact number, email, and delivery address when you place an order or contact us.
  • Communication details from WhatsApp, Messenger, or other direct message platforms.
  • Non‑personal data such as browser type, IP address, and device information for analytics and website improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders accurately and efficiently.

3. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, track preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • You may opt out of promotional messages by contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any revisions will be posted on this page and dated accordingly. Material changes may be communicated through our website or messaging channels.
